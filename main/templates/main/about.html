PURPOSE FOR DEVELOPING THIS PROJECT -



In the era of today's busy life, everyone is getting stressed because of certain reasons. And there is a great need for such a thing where a person can listen to music and enjoy it, can read novels and comics for entertainment. This definitely helps in reducing stress and makes them relaxed.
This is a project where a person gets three things at one place. You can enjoy listening music anytime. You can read comics and novels to refresh your mind.
Various benefits of music, novels and comics:-
-Research has shown that listening to music can reduce anxiety, blood pressure, and pain as well as improve sleep quality, mood, mental alertness, and memory.
-Reading novels and comics:-
 *improves brain connectivity.
 *increases your vocabulary and comprehension.
 *empowers you to empathize with other people.
 *aids in sleep readiness.
 *reduces stress.
 *lowers blood pressure and heart rate.
 *fights depression symptoms.
 *prevents cognitive decline as you age.
